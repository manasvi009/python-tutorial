# YouTube Video Search Commands

## 🎵 New Feature: YouTube Video Search and Play

Jarvis can now search for and play videos on YouTube based on your voice commands!

### 🎯 Supported Commands:

#### 1. **Play Specific Videos**
- "Jarvis, play music on YouTube"
- "Jarvis, play tutorials on YouTube" 
- "Jarvis, play funny videos on YouTube"
- "Jarvis, play cooking videos on YouTube"

#### 2. **Direct YouTube Commands**
- "Jarvis, youtube play songs"
- "Jarvis, youtube play news"
- "Jarvis, youtube play sports"

#### 3. **Search Commands**
- "Jarvis, search youtube for python tutorials"
- "Jarvis, search youtube for music"
- "Jarvis, search youtube for comedy"

#### 4. **Combined Commands**
- "Jarvis, play latest songs on youtube"
- "Jarvis, search youtube for how to cook pasta"
- "Jarvis, youtube play educational videos"

### 🚀 How It Works:
1. **Say "Jarvis"** to activate the assistant
2. **Give a YouTube command** like "play music on YouTube"
3. **Jarvis will:**
   - Search YouTube for your query
   - Open the search results in your browser
   - Show relevant videos
4. **You can then:**
   - Click on any video to play it
   - Use YouTube's interface to select videos
   - Browse through the search results

### 💡 Examples:
- **"Jarvis, play relaxing music on YouTube"** → Opens YouTube search for "relaxing music"
- **"Jarvis, search youtube for coding tutorials"** → Opens YouTube search for "coding tutorials"  
- **"Jarvis, youtube play funny cats"** → Opens YouTube search for "funny cats"

### 🎵 What You Can Search For:
- Music (songs, playlists, artists)
- Tutorials (cooking, coding, DIY)
- Entertainment (comedy, movies, shows)
- News and current events
- Educational content
- Sports highlights
- Gaming videos
- And much more!

### ⚠️ Important Notes:
- Jarvis opens the **search results page**, not individual videos
- You still need to **click on the video** you want to watch
- Works with any search query you can think of
- Requires internet connection to search YouTube

The assistant will now help you find exactly what you're looking for on YouTube through voice commands! 🎉

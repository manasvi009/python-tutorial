# Jarvis App Launcher Commands

## Applications You Can Open With Voice Commands

### Microsoft Office
- "open word" or "launch word"
- "open excel" or "launch excel" 
- "open powerpoint" or "launch powerpoint"

### System Tools
- "open notepad" or "launch notepad"
- "open calculator" or "launch calculator"
- "open paint" or "launch paint"
- "open cmd" or "launch cmd"
- "open command prompt" or "launch command prompt"
- "open powershell" or "launch powershell"
- "open task manager" or "launch task manager"
- "open control panel" or "launch control panel"
- "open settings" or "launch settings"
- "open explorer" or "launch explorer"
- "open file manager" or "launch file manager"

### Web Browsers
- "open chrome" or "launch chrome"
- "open firefox" or "launch firefox"
- "open edge" or "launch edge"

### Media & Entertainment
- "open vlc" or "launch vlc"
- "open spotify" or "launch spotify"
- "open steam" or "launch steam"

### Communication
- "open discord" or "launch discord"

### Development Tools
- "open vs code" or "launch vs code"
- "open code" or "launch code"
- "open sublime" or "launch sublime"

### Adobe Suite
- "open photoshop" or "launch photoshop"
- "open illustrator" or "launch illustrator"

## How to Use:
1. Say "Jarvis" to activate
2. Say any of the commands above
3. Examples:
   - "Jarvis, open notepad"
   - "Jarvis, launch calculator"
   - "Jarvis, start chrome"
   - "Jarvis, open word app"

## Alternative Command Formats:
- "open [app name]"
- "launch [app name]"
- "start [app name] app"
- "Jarvis, can you open [app name]?"

The assistant will try to find and launch the application. If it can't find a specific app, it will let you know!
